The most played game on steam this year so far is Palworld, appearing very early into the year it already took to the most popular game of early 2024
Popular for its Pokemon-Like appearance, Palworld is popular because of the controversy between it and pokemon, being in the making for a few years, people have wondered whether it would be immediately taken down by law suits from pokemon or not. Palworld is an upcoming action-adventure, survival, and monster-taming game created and published by Japanese developer Pocket Pair. The game is set in an open world populated with animal-like creatures called "Pals", which players can battle and capture to use for base building, traversal, and combat.
